Weekly Cohorts → In-Year Recognized Revenue

Each week, new booking cohorts arrive. Subscribers pay monthly, so each week’s cohort generates recognized revenue for the remaining months of the fiscal year. Earlier weeks produce more recognized revenue because their cohorts pay for more months.
